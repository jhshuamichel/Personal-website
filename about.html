Even though I am not the Tiger Woods of fishing I really enjoy fishing. Maybe it has to do with the combination of escaping the noise of the city and buildings.
There is something amazing about the ocean breeze and the stinky smell of the pier that just makes fishing a relaxing hobby of mine.
Freshwater fishing is probably my favorite. If you find a freshwater spot with clear water, that is the cherry on top. Being able to see the bass swim and look at your bait and take it.
